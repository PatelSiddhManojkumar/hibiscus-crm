✦ The one nobody else has

Copilot operates the CRM through its own tools.

Type an instruction in plain English. Copilot writes a plan, calls the same create-contact / move-deal / log-activity / run-report tools the UI uses, and shows its work step by step. It's not a chatbot bolted on — it drives the product.

› Move Q3 Bulk Order to negotiation and remind me to send the proforma tomorrow
◇ Two steps: advance the deal, then create a dated task.
→ move_deal {deal:"Q3 Bulk Order", stage:"negotiation"}
✓ Moved to Negotiation, probability now 70%
→ create_task {title:"Send proforma", due_in_days:1}
✓ Task created, due tomorrow
